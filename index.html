My Favorite Sith Lords

Formerly known as Anakin Skywalker, Darth Vader is now more machine than human. While intimidating and capable of great strength, Vader is still susceptible to force lightning and incapable underlings.

A force-sensitive Zabrak trained in the ways of the Sith, Darth Maul is especially skilled in lightsaber combat. During the Battle of Naboo, Padawan Kenobi severed the connection between Maul's legs and upper body.

An incredibly powerful force user, Darth Sidious is also a mastermind who managed to orchestrate the fall of the Republic and the Jedi Order. However, it is my humble opinion that he got lucky against Mace Windu.

Count Dooku was one of the chief generals during the Clone Wars, and squared off many times against Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi. Too bad he met his fate in the form of a lightsaber guillotine after being stabbed in the back by his master.
"Did you ever hear the tragedy of Darth Plagueis The Wise? I thought not. It’s not a story the Jedi would tell you. It’s a Sith legend. Darth Plagueis was a Dark Lord of the Sith, so powerful and so wise he could use the Force to influence the midichlorians to create life… He had such a knowledge of the dark side that he could even keep the ones he cared about from dying. The dark side of the Force is a pathway to many abilities some consider to be unnatural. He became so powerful… the only thing he was afraid of was losing his power, which eventually, of course, he did. Unfortunately, he taught his apprentice everything he knew, then his apprentice killed him in his sleep. Ironic. He could save others from death, but not himself."

- Sheev Palpatine
